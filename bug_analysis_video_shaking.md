# Bug Analysis: Video Shaking/Distortion in Generated Clips

## Issue
The generated clip video has visible shaking/distortion (画面の揺れ・歪み) during playback.

## Observations from frames
- The video is a clip generated by the AI clip generator (V2 pipeline)
- It shows a woman speaking with Japanese subtitles at the bottom
- The video player shows: left side `0:20 / 1:30` (actual video time), right side `00:00:02 / 01:00:00` (screen recording timer)
- The right side with "结束录制" (end recording) button indicates this is a screen recording of someone watching the clip
- The person recording is noting the shaking/distortion issue in the generated clip

## Root Cause Hypothesis
The shaking/distortion is likely caused by:
1. Video stabilization applied during clip generation (over-correction)
2. Or: the original video had movement and the clip extraction process introduced artifacts
3. Or: ffmpeg processing (scaling, cropping, or re-encoding) introduced the distortion

## Where to look in code
- `worker/batch/process_video.py` - main video processing pipeline
- `backend/app/api/v1/endpoints/ai_clip_generator.py` - clip generation code
- Any ffmpeg commands that apply stabilization or motion correction
